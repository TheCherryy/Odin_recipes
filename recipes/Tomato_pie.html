Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
  • 3 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Place tomato slices in a colander and sprinkle with salt. Let drain for 10 minutes; pat dry with paper towels.
  3. Arrange a single layer of tomato slices in the bottom of the pie crust. Top with half of the green onions and half of the Cheddar cheese. Repeat layering with remaining tomatoes, green onions, and Cheddar cheese.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, black pepper, oregano, basil, and garlic powder. Spread mayonnaise mixture evenly over the top of the pie.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly, about 30 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
